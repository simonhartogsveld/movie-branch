About the movie

Pulp Fiction (1994), directed by Quentin Tarantino, is a landmark crime film celebrated for its non-linear storytelling, dark humor, and sharp dialogue. It intertwines multiple storylines about hitmen, a troubled boxer, a powerful mob boss, and his enigmatic wife, creating a vivid tapestry of Los Angeles’ criminal underworld. Blending violence with irony, the film explores themes of loyalty, chance, and redemption while showcasing unforgettable characters, a striking soundtrack, and stylistic flourishes that reshaped modern cinema and influenced countless filmmakers worldwide..
